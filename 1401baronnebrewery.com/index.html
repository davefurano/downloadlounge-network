The story
The concept behind 1401 Baronne Brewery.

1401 Baronne Brewery is a New Orleans craft brewery concept rooted in the neighborhood culture of the city's Central City and Garden District neighborhoods. Real beer, real community, real New Orleans.

Every beer we make tells a New Orleans story — the streets, the culture, the history, and the people who make this city unlike anywhere else on earth. 1401 Baronne is where the neighborhood gathers.
