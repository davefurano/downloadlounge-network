The story
The concept behind Culinary Institute NOLA.

Culinary Institute NOLA is a culinary education institution built around the extraordinary food culture of New Orleans — teaching the techniques, history, and artistry behind the dishes that define one of the world's great food cities.

From the fundamentals of a proper roux to the art of a perfect étouffée, Culinary Institute NOLA teaches cooking the way it was meant to be learned — with real ingredients, real kitchens, and real New Orleans chefs who've spent their lives mastering the craft.
