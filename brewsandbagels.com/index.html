The story
The concept behind Brews and Bagels.

Brews and Bagels is a New Orleans concept built around two perfect things — hand-rolled, kettle-boiled bagels and carefully curated craft beer. Open early, unapologetically casual, and uncommonly good.

Most mornings deserve more than a drive-through. Brews and Bagels is the spot where the neighborhood gathers — with fresh bagels loaded with local ingredients, a rotating tap list, and the kind of coffee that actually wakes you up.

How AI powers Brews and Bagels
1
AI bagel demand forecasting
AI predicts daily bagel demand by variety so we bake exactly what sells — fresh every morning, never stale, never wasted.
2
Tap list optimization
AI rotates the tap list based on time of day, customer profiles, and seasonal demand — the right beer for the right morning.
3
Ingredient freshness tracking
AI monitors ingredient quality and shelf life — ensuring every bagel is made with the freshest possible components.
4
Customer loyalty AI
AI identifies regulars and their preferences — enabling personalized greetings, order predictions, and loyalty rewards.
