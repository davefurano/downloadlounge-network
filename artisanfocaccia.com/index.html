The story
The concept behind Artisan Focaccia.

Artisan Focaccia is a New Orleans focaccia concept built around the idea that great bread deserves to be the star of the show. Hand-made, slow-fermented, locally sourced, and unlike anything else in the city.

We believe focaccia is the perfect food — crispy on the outside, pillowy in the middle, endlessly versatile, and deeply satisfying. We're bringing the best version of it to New Orleans, with local flavors woven into every loaf.
